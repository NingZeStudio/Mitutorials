# External Download Resources for Minecraft (Browser Channels Only)

If you can't find the resources you need within the launcher, you can download them from the following browser channels and import them manually.

## How to Download Mods?

### Modrinth

[Modrinth](https://modrinth.com/mods) — An open-source-friendly mod platform with a great search and download experience.

### CurseForge

[CurseForge](https://www.curseforge.com/minecraft/mc-mods) — The largest mod hosting platform with the most comprehensive resources.

### Other Channels

- **Aifadian (爱发电)** — Some Chinese authors publish mods here; search on your own

## How to Download Resource Packs?

- [Modrinth - Resource Packs](https://modrinth.com/resourcepacks)
- [CurseForge - Resource Packs](https://www.curseforge.com/minecraft/texture-packs)
- Aifadian and others (search on your own)

## How to Download Shaders?

- [Modrinth - Shaders](https://modrinth.com/shaders)
- [CurseForge - Shaders](https://www.curseforge.com/minecraft/shaders)
- Aifadian and others (search on your own)

## How to Download Modpacks?

- [Modrinth - Modpacks](https://modrinth.com/modpacks)
- [CurseForge - Modpacks](https://www.curseforge.com/minecraft/modpacks)
- Aifadian and others (search on your own)
